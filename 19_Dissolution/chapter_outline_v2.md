# Chapter 19: Dissolution - V2

## Chapter Summary
**REVISED WITH NEW ENDING**: After Liz's death, Simera exists in fragments. Days or weeks pass—time has lost meaning. The funeral, arrangements, aftermath—all blur together. Simera's mental state, already fragile from Memento use, completely fractures after losing Liz. The guilt is crushing: she failed to save Liz, enabled her addiction, didn't see the signs, let the woman she loved die while she watched. Her OCD spirals completely out of control—counting everything obsessively but numbers mean nothing. Reality becomes less and less solid. She can't distinguish memories of Liz from Memento experiences. Sometimes unsure if Liz is really dead or if this is a Memento. Sometimes doesn't know who she is. The fragmentation is nearly complete. Throughout, Simera's deterioration shown visibly—not just grief but accumulated damage from addiction, fractured sense of self, inability to distinguish real from recorded. One day—weeks after Liz's death—Simera stands on the roof of a building. The same building from Chapter 1? Maybe. She stands at the edge. **This time it's real. She is wearing a hospital gown. She has her recording device.** This will be her final Memento—she'll record her own death and it will sell. Money she'll never see, but it's all she has left to give. She activates the device. Plugs it in. Hits record. Steps off. The fall is real. Long, terrifying, no beauty, just horror and disconnection. She's dying, really dying. At the last moment before impact, she sees her reflection in a puddle below—her own face, finally, but she doesn't recognize it. **NEW ENDING: Impact. Then—we cut to someone else. A stranger. Waking up in their bed, pale, sweating, gasping. Just like Simera did in Chapter 1. They just experienced Simera's death. The Memento of her suicide. On their phone, the Memento is paused at the final frame: Simera's reflection about to break. The title: "The Fall - Final Recording." Posted by Heri Corporation. When Simera signed the creator contract, she gave Heri rights to keep and market ALL her recordings. Including this one. Her death is now content. Someone just paid money to experience her suicide. The circle is complete. The system continues. Heri profits even from her death.**
