# Chapter 9: Searching For Hope - V2

## Chapter Summary
**REVISED**: Liz has a severe episode with her chronic stomach pain—worse than usual, debilitating. Despite their savings from Chapter 8, the medical costs are beyond what they've accumulated. The emergency drains their savings completely and then some. Insurance gaps, specialist costs, procedures they can't fully afford. Simera sees her suffering and feels helpless, desperate. The stability from Chapter 8 evaporates in days. Back to the edge, back to calculations, back to desperation. The support group plans are shelved—can't help others when drowning yourself. Simera knows what sells best: extreme danger. She calculates what she'd need to make and how fast. The pressure mounts toward the dangerous recording in Chapter 10.
