CHAPTER 9: FRACTURES
=====================

CHAPTER PITCH:
Simera performs her first no-safety-equipment fall from sixty stories onto a net that catches her with brutal impact that breaks two ribs and fractures her collarbone, but her intensity rating hits a perfect 10.0 and Heri pays her 43,000 units, and when she returns home bloodied and exultant, Liz sees the madness blazing in her girlfriend's eyes and finally says what she's been avoiding—"You're going to die, and you want to die, and I can't watch anymore"—and in the terrible silence that follows, Simera realizes that Liz is right, that she does want to die, has wanted it all along, and that the only kindness left is to let Liz go before the inevitable ending, so she says "Then leave," and Liz does, walking out with a small bag while Simera sits alone in their apartment, ribs screaming, finally feeling something that isn't falling.

FINAL NOTE:
This is the chapter where Simera loses the last thing tethering her to life. Liz's departure is both liberation and abandonment. The perfect 10.0 rating is achievement and condemnation—nowhere higher to go except into death itself. End with Simera alone, broken, and paradoxically free. The final chapters will accelerate toward the inevitable conclusion. She has nothing left to live for, which means nothing stopping her from dying.
