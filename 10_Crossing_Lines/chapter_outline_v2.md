# Chapter 10: Crossing Lines - V2

## Chapter Summary
**REVISED ENDING**: Simera breaks. With Liz in severe pain and medical bills mounting, Simera makes the decision. She doesn't tell Liz—she can't. She researches the most profitable Mementos: something involving real physical risk, extreme danger. The preparation is detailed. She's terrified. The recording session itself is the heart of the chapter: visceral, detailed, dangerous, the fear is real, but so is the intensity. When it's over, she's shaking, alive, changed—and disturbed by how good it felt. She gets substantial money. Helps Liz, pays bills. Relief floods through both of them. **CHANGED: Liz discovers what Simera did—finding evidence or seeing injuries. But her reaction is horror and overwhelming guilt, not anger. She realizes Simera risked death FOR HER. The guilt crushes Liz: "I'm the burden destroying you." This drives Liz into depression and self-blame, spiraling into seeing herself as the problem. The chapter ends with Liz's guilt and depression, setting up her breaking in Chapter 11.**
