# Chapter 18: The Breaking Point - V2

## Chapter Summary
**REVISED**: Time passes after Chapter 17—days, maybe weeks. The pattern continues: Liz using constantly, increasingly extreme content, Simera unable to stop her. They exist in exhausted crisis. Then: **Simera comes home. The apartment is too quiet. She calls for Liz. No answer. She finds her in the bedroom. Liz is on the bed, convulsing. Foam at her mouth. The device still plugged into the port behind her ear. The scene is terrifying, visceral—the nightmare they feared.** Simera screams, rushes to her, pulls the device out, tries to help. Liz's body is seizing. Her eyes are open but unseeing. This is overdose, poisoning, something catastrophic happening in her brain. Simera calls emergency services, hands shaking, mind fracturing. She tries to do what they tell her—turn Liz on her side, clear airway, keep her breathing. **But Liz dies.** Before the ambulance arrives, or in the ambulance, or at the hospital—but Liz dies. The love of Simera's life is gone. Through the horror, Simera sees Liz's phone. The screen shows a transaction: black market Memento purchased, modified/corrupted content, warnings ignored. The file suggests something extreme, dangerous, illegal. Liz bought it, used it, and it killed her. The chapter ends with Liz dead and Simera shattered. The guilt: "I should have known, should have seen, should have stopped her." Everything is broken. This is the point where Simera's mental state begins final descent toward Chapter 19.
