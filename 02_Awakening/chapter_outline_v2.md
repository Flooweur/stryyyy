## World-Building Details to Establish
- **Mementos**: 30 years old, majority of world population uses them
- **Technology**: Jack port behind ear (installed at birth now), feels exactly like reality
- **Heri Corporation**: Monopoly like Apple, handles everything
- **Legal status**: Legal and widespread, but editing is illegal (black market)
- **Side effects**: Dissociation, memory loss/blur with prolonged use
- **The Experience**: Users don't know they're in a Memento while experiencing it
- **Creator economy**: Exists but not fully explained yet (sets up later chapters)
