# Chapter 14: The Asking - V2

## Chapter Summary
**REVISED**: The role reversal is complete. Liz is now the one constantly asking Simera for Mementos. "Want to do one with me?" becomes a relentless question—morning, afternoon, evening, night. Sometimes it's Liz initiating, suggesting they use together. At first, Simera agrees, thinking it's still shared experience, still maintaining connection. But she begins to notice: Liz asks more often than she does. Liz chooses more extreme content every time. Liz can't seem to go a few hours without suggesting it. **The principled woman who refused to touch Mementos now needs them constantly.** Simera has her own addiction, struggles with her own cravings, but watching Liz deteriorate is different—terrifying. She sees what she couldn't see in herself. Liz is fragmenting faster. Her personality is disappearing. The absurdist humor that defined her is gone, replaced by desperate need for escape. Simera realizes with horror: Liz is worse off than she is. Liz is the addict now. The roles have completely reversed, and Simera doesn't know how to save someone from something she's still caught in herself.
