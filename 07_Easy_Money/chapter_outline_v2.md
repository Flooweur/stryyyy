# Chapter 7: Easy Money - V2

## Chapter Summary
Simera creates a falling Memento (relatively safe height - maybe 20 stories). It sells significantly better than the walking tour. Money starts flowing - not wealthy, but breathing room for the first time in months. Bills get paid. A few more successful Mementos follow. But the pressure is always there: the algorithm showing that more extreme content pays even better. **NEW: Simera encounters Ed again - he seems happier, tells her he's happy she's succeeding, says he has a "pretty wild idea" for content but won't reveal what.** The meeting feels positive but ominous. Meanwhile, Liz watches with concern as Simera gets more comfortable with creation, notices small changes. Success feels fragile but real. For a moment, it seems sustainable.
